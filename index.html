VSFS Virtual Internships at the VA

Intern with the Digital Media Engagement Team at the VA to Power Up Your Resume with Work Experience at a Federal Agency! The Digital Media Engagement (DME) interns team at the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) has over 250 student interns in various roles from social media content creation to graphic design, web design, web development, human resources, and team leadership. We write and design for official VA social media outlets as well as this web site.

  • Visit DMEInterns.org

About

The VA DME team has leveraged the skills and talents of hundreds of interns to become one of the U.S. federal government’s most successful student intern teams with a primary focus on social media content production including writing, graphic design, editing, and video production for the VA’s main social media outlets.
